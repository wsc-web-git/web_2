PCM • A

Engineering & Technology

STRONG Math 6 Years
  • •CS/IT, AI & Data Science
  • •Mechanical, Civil, EXTC, Electrical
  • •Entrance: MHT-CET / JEE
GOVT
₹6-8L
PRIVATE
₹10-15L+

Overview

Most preferred PCM path. 4-year B.E/B.Tech after HSC. Mumbai-Pune colleges have 90%+ placements for CS/IT. Core branches need GATE for PSUs.

Timeline Breakdown

HSC (2 yrs) + B.Tech (4 yrs) = 6 Years total. Optional M.Tech +2 = 8 Years.

Fees Mumbai Region 2026

  • Govt/Aided (VJTI, SPIT, KJ Somaiya Aided): ₹80,000 - ₹1,00,000 per year
  • Private (DJ Sanghvi, Thadomal, NMIMS Mukesh Patel): ₹1.5L - 2.5L per year
  • Total 4-yr degree: Govt ₹3.2-4L, Private ₹7-10L
  • HSC + JEE/MHT-CET Coaching (Allen, Pace, Rao): ₹2-4L for 2 years
  • Hostel Mumbai: ₹1.2-1.8L per year extra

Sub-Careers with Exact Timelines

  • CS/IT, AI & DS: 6 yrs, Avg package ₹8-15L
  • Mechanical: 6 yrs, Core + MBA option
  • Civil: 6 yrs, Govt jobs via MPSC
  • EXTC/Electronics: 6 yrs, merges with IT/software
  • Electrical: 6 yrs, PSUs via GATE

PCM • B

Architecture & Design

STRONG Math 7 Years
  • •B.Arch (5 yrs) + M.Arch optional
  • •B.Des in UI/UX, Product, Interior
  • •Entrance: NATA, JEE Paper 2
GOVT
₹4-6L
PRIVATE
₹8-15L+

Details

B.Arch is 5 years (2+5=7 total with HSC). B.Des is 4 years (2+4=6 total). PCM with good drawing skills required.

  • Govt Sir J.J College: ₹70k/yr = ₹3.5L total
  • Private Rachana Sansad, DY Patil: ₹1.5-3L/yr = ₹8-15L+
  • NATA/JEE Paper 2 Coaching Mumbai: ₹40,000 - ₹80,000
  • Portfolio & software (AutoCAD, Sketchup) needed

PCB • C

Medicine & Surgery

WEAK/BASIC Math 7.5+ Years
  • •MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS, BVSc
  • •NEET is mandatory + Biology focus
  • •Longest but most respected
GOVT
₹8-12L
PRIVATE
₹45-80L+

NEET Path

MBBS = 5.5 yrs (incl internship) + HSC 2 = 7.5 yrs. With PG (MD/MS 3 yrs) = 10.5+ yrs.

  • NEET Coaching Mumbai (Aakash, Allen, Lakshya): ₹2-5L for 2 years
  • Govt MBBS (GMC Mumbai, LTMMC, Cooper): ₹1-1.2L/yr = ₹6-7L + HSC ₹2L = ₹8-12L total
  • Private (DY Patil, MGM, Terna): ₹9-15L/yr = ₹45-75L total
  • Management Quota: ₹80L - 1.2Cr total — avoid unless financially strong
  • BDS 5 yrs, BAMS/BHMS 5.5 yrs, BVSc 5.5 yrs — lower fees, faster practice
